# Distill — Crystallize the Work

This is the primary growth mechanism of the operating system. Real power comes from turning lived experience into durable, versioned, shareable intelligence.

**Core rule**: Only durable, non-derivable facts and patterns survive. Noise, one-off details, and things that can be looked up elsewhere are discarded.

---

## When to Use This Skill

- After a high-value session or piece of work
- When you want to turn a repeated pattern into a first-class skill or command
- After client work, strategy, or debugging marathons where real insight emerged
- As the closing move of any significant Raven or personal flow (pairs with r-close / o-close equivalents)
- When building the long-term memory surface (replaces the old heavy MEMORY.md accretion habit)

---

## The Discipline

1. **Start with intent**: What is the reusable essence here? For whom? In what future context will this save time or prevent error?
2. **Extract only what travels**: 
   - Named patterns and their triggers
   - Decision frameworks with clear inputs/outputs
   - Specific gotchas + the exact conditions under which they appear
   - Contracts, templates, or checklists that are actually useful
   - References to deeper source material (don't duplicate)
3. **Ruthless anti-bloat**:
   - No "this one time we..." stories unless they illustrate a general rule
   - No raw conversation logs
   - No facts that are easily discoverable elsewhere (use links or precise citations)
4. **Output form** (choose the highest-leverage one):
   - New or updated Skill (most common)
   - Agent / persona definition or prompt profile
   - Project or global AGENTS.md addition
   - Client brief / runbook
   - Memory reference document (lightweight)
   - Command shim

---

## Recommended Flow (Grok Native)

- Invoke this skill at the end of meaningful work (or when the pattern has repeated 2–3 times).
- Feed it the relevant session summary, key artifacts, or the problem + solution.
- The skill will propose a tight, high-signal artifact.
- You review + refine.
- It gets written to the right place (`~/.grok/skills/`, project `.grok/`, or a memory reference).

This is how the surface grows without turning into the old 2 GB blob.

---

## Relationship to Other Parts of the OS

- Works beautifully after `calcinate` (you just burned a bunch of rot — distill the lessons).
- Works beautifully after MARS-style synthesis (the cross-domain insights become durable frameworks).
- Is the natural closer for `raven-prime` / `raven-topology` heavy sessions (turn operational learning into fleet intelligence that persists).
- Feeds the global doctrine in `~/.grok/AGENTS.md` when a principle is stress-tested in reality.
